About us

This new iteration of Bluesville pays homage to the original Bluesville imprint launched byÂ Prestige Records. The original imprint was created to preserve the blues genre and highlight its key living figures. Now, Bluesville Records seeks to elevate the legacies of the artists behind the albums and honor their immeasurable impact on modern music.

As part of Craft Recordings, Bluesville Records sources its selections from the celebrated catalog of iconic labels such as these.
